Simple process

How to make a booking

Three steps from login to confirmed reservation.

1
login

Log in to your account

Use your APTS credentials. Faculty, staff, students, and missionaries all have their own portal. Contact the admin office if you need access.

2
event_available

Pick a space and date

Browse rooms filtered by building, type, and availability. Choose your dates, add guest count, and submit your request — including group bookings for multiple rooms.

3
mark_email_read

Get your confirmation

The facilities office reviews and approves your booking. You'll see the status update in your dashboard and receive a confirmation email once approved.
