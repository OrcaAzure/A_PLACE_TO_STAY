How to make a booking

From sign-in to payment — housing staff review every request before it’s confirmed.

1
login

Sign in

Use your APTS account, or guest access issued by Housing for approved visitors.

2
calendar_month

Choose dates & space

Browse GMC rooms or campus venues, check live availability, and submit single or group requests.

3
fact_check

Staff review

Facilities and housing teams approve lodging and venue requests. Track status in your dashboard.

4
receipt_long

Pay & confirm

Once approved, complete payment (GCash, cash, or bank transfer) and receive your invoice by email.
