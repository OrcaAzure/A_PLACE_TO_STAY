How to make a booking

From browse to payment — housing staff review every request before it’s confirmed.

1
explore

Browse & pick a space

Open Browse to see GMC rooms or campus venues, check live availability, and choose what fits your stay.

2
calendar_month

Choose dates & submit

Set your dates, add guests or event details, and submit single or group requests from My Stays.

3
fact_check

Staff review

Facilities and housing teams approve lodging and venue requests. Track status in My Stays.

4
receipt_long

Pay & confirm

Once approved, complete payment (GCash, cash, or bank transfer) and receive your invoice by email.
