Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

APTS — A Place to Stay is operated by Asia Pacific Theological Seminary to manage campus housing and facility bookings. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you use the APTS portal.

Information we collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, and role (guest, resident, or staff).
  • Booking and reservation data, including dates, facilities, and payment references where applicable.
  • Technical logs needed to secure the service (IP address, browser type, and session activity).

How we use information

  • Process lodging and facility reservations and communicate booking status.
  • Coordinate campus access, billing, and support with authorized APTS staff.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve the reliability of APTS.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell personal data. Information is shared only with APTS departments that need it to fulfill your booking or comply with campus policy. Records are retained as required for operations, accounting, and legal obligations, then deleted or anonymized when no longer needed.

Your choices

You may update profile details in Account Settings or contact facilities staff to request access, correction, or deletion of data where permitted by law and campus policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: guestservices@apts.edu or visit APTS contact.
